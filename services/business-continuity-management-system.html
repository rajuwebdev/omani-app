Our BCM Framework
1. Business Impact Analysis (BIA)

We identify:

  • Critical business functions
  • Recovery Time Objectives (RTO)
  • Recovery Point Objectives (RPO)
  • Financial & operational impact of downtime

This ensures recovery priorities are based on measurable business impact — not assumptions.

2. Risk Assessment

We evaluate:

  • Cyber threats
  • IT failures
  • Cloud disruptions
  • Vendor dependencies
  • Facility risks
  • Regulatory exposure

Risk-based continuity planning ensures practical and defensible resilience.

3. Business Continuity Strategy Design

We define:

  • Alternate site strategies
  • Backup architecture
  • Workforce continuity planning
  • Crisis communication framework
  • Third-party continuity integration

Every strategy is tailored to your operational structure.

4. Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Plan Development

We develop:

  • Enterprise Business Continuity Plan (BCP)
  • IT Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP)
  • Crisis Management Plan
  • Incident Response Alignment
  • Communication Playbooks

All documentation is audit-ready and regulator-aligned.

5. Testing & Simulation

Plans that are not tested will fail. We conduct:

  • Tabletop exercises
  • Technical DR simulations
  • Cyber disruption scenarios
  • Executive crisis simulations

Testing validates readiness and builds leadership confidence.

6. Continuous Monitoring & Improvement

BCM is not a one-time project. Our ongoing service includes:

  • Annual BIA updates
  • Plan revisions
  • Regulatory alignment updates
  • Audit preparation support
  • KPI & resilience reporting to management
